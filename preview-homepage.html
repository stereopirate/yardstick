What Changed
1
Section Dividers
"Weather & Conditions", "Your Plan", "Quick Actions" labels break the page into scannable groups.
2
Weather Metric Icons
Each metric tile now has a small SVG icon (💧 humidity, wind lines, 🌧 rain, ☂ rain %) — clearer than bare text labels.
3
Season Banner — Colored Tint
Background now has a subtle season-color tint, icon sits in a rounded container, label and action pill use the season accent color.
4
Advisory Card — Status Border
Entire card gets a left border + header tint matching most urgent status. Red=HOLD, amber=CAUTION, green=GOOD. Color dot in header reinforces signal.
5
Priority Tasks — Pill Badges
Replaced emoji dots (🟢🟡🔴) with typed pill badges: Go Soon Hold. Task rows use a gray row background.
6
Activity Cards — Color Left Border
Each card now has a 4px left border in the activity's brand color (green=mowing, orange=fertilizer, blue=watering). Replaces the small color dot.
