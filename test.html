Research-Backed Lawn Care

Your lawn, coached by science—not guesswork.

Most lawn care apps are built by marketing companies looking to sell you a subscription box. **Yardstick is different.** We are a free, privacy-first tool designed for the homeowner who wants professional results using land-grant university research.

We believe that the best lawn care happens when you understand the "why" behind the "when." By combining your local soil temperatures with a database of 110+ extension publications, Yardstick gives you a custom roadmap for your specific grass type and climate zone.

No Login. No Tracking.

Your data is yours. Yardstick stores everything locally on your device. No accounts to create, no passwords to reset, and no data sold to third parties.

University-Backed
