Finish setting up your lawn profile

To unlock your personalized care program, Yardstick needs a few quick details about your lawn. It only takes a minute — and once it's done, everything in the app is tailored to you.

  • 1 Select your grass type Choose from 6 supported grass types — Bermuda, Zoysia, St. Augustine, Tall Fescue, Kentucky Bluegrass, and more. Not sure? The app helps you identify it.
  • 2 Enter your zip code Yardstick auto-detects your USDA hardiness zone (4–9) so your care schedule matches your actual climate, not a one-size-fits-all calendar.
  • 3 Review your Full Year Program Once your grass and zone are set, your 12-month care schedule is ready — fertilizing windows, seeding timing, mow height adjustments, and more.
  • 4 Add your equipment (optional) Log your mower from 75+ models. Yardstick tracks hours and tells you when maintenance is due — oil changes, blade sharpening, and more.
